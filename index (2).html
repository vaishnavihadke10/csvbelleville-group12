School Programs

Street Safety Program

Our most popular school program — navigate real traffic lights, practice 911 calls, and learn pedestrian safety skills in our miniature city. Fully aligned with Ontario curriculum for JK–Grade 4.

What's Included

Students take on the role of pedestrians in our fully functioning miniature town — complete with working traffic signals, railway crossing arms, road signs, and a real phone booth for practising 911 calls. Each session is led by a trained CSV educator alongside a community partner.

🚦

Working Traffic Lights

Practice crossing with real signals and crossing guard cues.

📞

911 Practice Calls

Use our working phone booth to practice real emergency calls.

🚂

Railway Crossing Arms

Learn safe behaviour at rail crossings with functioning barriers.

👮

Community Partners

Police, fire, or EMS officers lead interactive discussions.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of the program, students will be able to:

  • ✓ Safely cross streets and intersections
  • ✓ Read and respond to traffic signals
  • ✓ Call 911 and give key information
  • ✓ Identify safe adults and safe spaces
  • ✓ Understand railway crossing rules
  • ✓ Practise bus safety procedures

Curriculum Alignment

Street Safety is designed in partnership with HPEDSB and meets expectations in Ontario's Health and Physical Education curriculum, as well as Social Studies and Language for Grades JK–4. All CSV programs are delivered free of charge to school groups and are fully adaptable for students of all abilities.
